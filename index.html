About Me

Howdy! Thanks for checking out my webpage. My name is Joey Stanley, and I am a 23 year old Durham native and recent Duke graduate. I received a degree in Computer Science and will be working as a software engineer full time beginning in late September. While I am not working, I am usually going fishing, playing golf or soccer, or out in the woods hiking through nature. I am someone who likes to get out there and have fun, and I am always looking forward to my next adventure.

Although I graduated from Duke University with a degree in Computer Science, I have always had a passion for teaching and caring for children. Before going to college, I worked as a babysitter, tutor, and summer camp counselor. I also began working as a K-8th grade after school teacher at Immaculata Catholic School. After working at Immaculata from 2020-2022, I began working twice a week as a DPS high school classroom teacher. Through these various roles I have learned how to work as a role model for kids of all ages while making sure they have a safe, fun time.

I am open to nannying (or any other type of work) until September 13th when I start my job. If you need a babysitter for a day, nanny for a month, or want someone to give your kid lessons in school, sports, or fun... shoot me a text :)
